Complaints process

We respond quickly and transparently

If you have concerns about our service, please follow the steps below. We commit to timely acknowledgement and resolution.

1

Submit

Email medcomms@tanityglobal.com with your project reference and concern.

2

Acknowledge

We acknowledge within one business day and assign a responsible contact.

3

Review

We investigate with the consulting team, propose remediation, and target resolution within seven business days.

4

Escalate

If unresolved, escalate to our lead consultant for independent review. Final written response provided.

Our promise

  • Transparent communication and documented steps
  • Options to adjust scope or provide additional clarification
  • Respect for confidentiality and data security
  • No retaliation for raising concerns
