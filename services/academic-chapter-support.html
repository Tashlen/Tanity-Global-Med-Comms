Who this service is for

  • Postgraduate students drafting or revising thesis chapters
  • Researchers aligning multi-author chapters
  • Clinicians structuring service evaluation reports
  • Supervisors seeking structured feedback for candidates

What we assist with

  • Literature review flow and synthesis
  • Methods chapter clarity and alignment with aims
  • Results presentation, tables, and figures
  • Discussion framing, limitations, and next steps
  • Reference management and formatting checks

Quality, ethics & confidentiality

We respect authorship and institutional guidelines, keep drafts confidential, and avoid rewriting entire chapters on your behalf.

Frequently asked questions

Do you edit or rewrite?

We edit for clarity and structure, not ghostwrite. You remain the author.

Can you match my citation style?

Yes, we align to APA, Vancouver, Harvard, or specified institutional styles.

How detailed is feedback?

We provide inline comments, margin notes, and a summary of priorities.

Do you help with supervisor feedback?

We interpret and plan responses while respecting their academic role.

Is the process collaborative?

Yes, we schedule touchpoints to ensure the narrative stays yours.
