Qualitative Data Interpretation

Translate narratives into credible insights

We guide coding frameworks, thematic analysis, and credibility checks for qualitative studies.

Request a Consultation Schedule a Call

Who this service is for

  • Researchers conducting interviews, focus groups, or field notes
  • Clinicians capturing patient narratives for service improvement
  • Students refining qualitative methodology chapters
  • Teams integrating qualitative findings into mixed-methods designs

What we assist with

  • Codebook development and inter-coder alignment
  • Thematic, content, or framework analysis guidance
  • Credibility checks: member checking, triangulation, audit trails
  • Reflexivity statements and positionality reflections
  • Clear reporting aligned to SRQR and COREQ expectations

How the process works

1

Initial consultation

Review research questions, participants, and data collection approach.

2

Scope alignment

Choose analysis frameworks and agree on coding strategies.

3

Expert input & feedback

Iterate codebooks, exemplar quotes, and thematic maps.

4

Final review & next-step guidance

Deliver reporting outlines and credibility checklists for defense.

Quality, ethics & confidentiality

We protect participant anonymity, encourage reflexivity, and avoid overstating thematic claims beyond the data.

Frequently asked questions

Do you code transcripts for me?

We guide and review coding; primary coding remains with you or your team.

Can you use my preferred software?

We support common tools (NVivo, ATLAS.ti, Excel) and focus on transparent processes.

How do you handle bilingual data?

We plan translation checks and preserve meaning through dual coding when possible.

Do you help with participant quotes?

Yes, we help select illustrative, de-identified quotations.

Can you combine with quant findings?

We assist with integration frameworks for mixed-methods discussions.

Tell the story with integrity

We’ll help you surface themes responsibly and transparently.
