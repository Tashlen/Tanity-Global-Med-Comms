Systematic Reviews & Meta-Analyses

Plan and report evidence syntheses with rigor

We guide protocols, search strategies, screening workflows, and synthesis to meet PRISMA expectations.

Request a Consultation Schedule a Call

Who this service is for

  • Postgraduate students planning evidence syntheses
  • Clinicians comparing interventions or diagnostics
  • Researchers submitting PROSPERO or journal protocols
  • Teams coordinating multi-reviewer screening

What we assist with

  • Protocol development and registration guidance
  • Database search strategies and grey literature planning
  • Screening workflows and inter-rater reliability approaches
  • Data extraction forms and risk-of-bias assessments
  • Qualitative synthesis or meta-analysis plans with reporting templates

How the process works

1

Initial consultation

Clarify review question, inclusion criteria, and timelines.

2

Scope alignment

Design search strings, databases, and screening roles.

3

Expert input & feedback

Iterate protocols, pilot screening, and data extraction tools.

4

Final review & next-step guidance

Deliver PRISMA-aligned reporting templates and synthesis outline.

Quality, ethics & confidentiality

We promote transparent search documentation, dual screening, and bias assessment. All reference libraries and data stay under your control.

Frequently asked questions

Do you run the database searches?

We design and peer-review strategies; you or your librarian execute searches to keep access compliant.

Can you help with meta-analysis?

We guide model choices, effect calculations, and heterogeneity checks; execution is collaborative.

Which tools do you support?

We work with Covidence, Rayyan, or spreadsheet workflows depending on access.

Do you ensure no duplication?

We assist with deduplication steps and document the process.

Can you join as a co-author?

We can discuss contribution levels; authorship remains transparent and ethical.
