Who this service is for

  • Researchers selecting study designs and sampling frames
  • Clinicians running service evaluations or observational studies
  • Students refining methodology chapters
  • Teams planning multicenter or collaborative projects

What we assist with

  • Choosing study design and justification
  • Sampling strategies, power and effect considerations
  • Measurement tools, instruments, and validation
  • Bias mitigation and data quality protocols
  • Alignment to CONSORT, STROBE, SRQR, or relevant standards

How the process works

1

Initial consultation

Clarify objectives, population, resources, and ethical parameters.

2

Scope alignment

Select candidate designs and agree on constraints.

3

Expert input & feedback

Develop detailed methodology and data management plans.

4

Final review & next-step guidance

Deliver structured methodology text and implementation checklist.

Quality, ethics & confidentiality

We emphasise ethical approvals, participant protection, and data security. We document assumptions clearly to aid reproducibility.
