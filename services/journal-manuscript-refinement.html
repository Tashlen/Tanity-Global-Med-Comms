Frequently asked questions

Can you promise acceptance after revisions?

No. We strengthen clarity and responsiveness without guaranteeing outcomes.

Do you rewrite reviewer responses?

We co-draft responses and highlight evidence; you approve final wording.

Can you adjust analysis?

We advise on analytic clarifications; substantive re-analysis is scoped separately.

How do you track changes?

We provide tracked edits and a change log for transparency.

Do you liaise with co-authors?

We can coordinate feedback summaries and align contributions.
