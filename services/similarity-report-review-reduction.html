Interpret similarity responsibly

We help you read similarity reports, separate legitimate overlap from risk, and revise ethically.

Who this service is for

  • Students reviewing Turnitin or iThenticate reports
  • Researchers revising manuscripts for resubmission
  • Supervisors guiding candidates on originality
  • Clinicians preparing conference abstracts

What we assist with

  • Interpretation of similarity percentages and match types
  • Identifying legitimate citations vs. problematic overlap
  • Strategies for paraphrasing without altering meaning
  • Reference checks and quotation marking
  • Action plans and before/after examples for revisions

How the process works

1

Initial consultation

Review the report, thresholds, and institutional policies.

2

Scope alignment

Prioritise sections and define acceptable overlap parameters.

3

Expert input & feedback

Annotate matches, suggest paraphrasing, and refine citations.

4

Final review & next-step guidance

Provide a revision checklist and tips for future drafting.

Quality, ethics & confidentiality

We uphold originality, discourage over-reliance on paraphrasing tools, and keep your documents secure. We never claim to “clear” reports or guarantee scores.

Frequently asked questions

Can you reduce my similarity to a specific number?

We cannot guarantee a number; we focus on ethical improvement.

Do you edit the document?

We suggest changes and examples; you implement revisions to retain authorship.

What files do you need?

We review the report plus your document to understand context.

Will you paraphrase for me?

We model paraphrasing and cite appropriately; we do not rewrite entire sections.

How fast is the process?

Most reviews complete within 2–3 business days depending on length.
